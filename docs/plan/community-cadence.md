# Community and Release Cadence

## Monthly cadence

- Week 1: content proposals triage and roadmap prioritization
- Week 2: quality upgrades for existing packs (gold tier target)
- Week 3: new pack publishing and editorial reviews
- Week 4: release cut, notes, and impact review

## Labels and contribution flow

- `content`: new problem proposals and taxonomy updates
- `editorial`: explanation quality improvements
- `tests`: validator and expected-output fixes
- `good first issue`: beginner-friendly scoped tasks

## Delivery standards

- Every release includes at least one content quality improvement.
- CI and quality gates must be green before release tags.
- Release notes must include Added/Changed/Validation sections.
