# FAQ and Troubleshooting

## Why does browser runtime differ from local?

Browser stats are advisory and depend on hardware/runtime constraints.

## Why are some tests hidden?

Hidden tests prevent overfitting and brute-force-only submissions.

## How do I rebuild the web catalog?

```bash
python3 scripts/build_web_catalog.py
```

## How do I report a broken test?

Open a `Test or Validation Bug` issue from the repository templates.
