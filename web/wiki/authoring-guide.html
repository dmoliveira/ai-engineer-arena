Authoring Guide

Create educational problems that teach, not just test.

Create a new problem

python3 scripts/new_problem.py --id sample-problem --title "Sample Problem" --category algorithms --difficulty easy

Authoring checklist

  • Write a clear statement with realistic constraints.
  • Include at least 1 public test and hidden regression/stress tests.
  • Add editorial trade-offs and complexity notes.
  • Tag interview relevance and recommended approach.

Validate before commit

make validate
make quality
make build-catalog
Quality gate target: minimum score 70 and gold-tier baseline enforced in CI.
