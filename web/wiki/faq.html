FAQ and Troubleshooting

Why does browser runtime differ from local?

Browser stats are advisory and depend on device/runtime conditions.

Why hidden tests?

To reduce brute-force overfitting and evaluate robustness.

How to rebuild catalog?

python3 scripts/build_web_catalog.py

Why does a problem not run in browser?

Some tracks (shell) are terminal-first by design. For Python tracks, verify public test payload exists in web/public-tests/.

How do I report a content bug?

Use the issue templates in the repository (`Test or Validation Bug` or `Editorial Improvement`).
